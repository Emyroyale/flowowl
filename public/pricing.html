Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card for the free plan?
No. The free plan requires no credit card. Sign up and start a session immediately.
Can I cancel Pro at any time?
Yes. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account settings. No cancellation fees.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
The free plan lets you try FlowSpaceFocus before upgrading. Use it to get comfortable with the app, then upgrade when you want the AI coach and longer sessions.
What payment methods are accepted?
Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Major credit and debit cards are accepted.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your session history remains accessible. You lose access to Pro features but your account stays active on the free plan.
