How FlowSpaceFocus Works

FlowSpaceFocus is a web-based AI focus app that guides you through focused work sessions using music, timed sessions, and an AI coach. Here is exactly how it works, step by step.

1

Step 1

Choose your goal

Before every session, you set one specific goal. The AI coach helps you make it concrete. Vague intentions become focused commitments — so you start with a clear target instead of a fuzzy sense of "being productive."

2

Step 2

Pick your playlist

Choose from 8 focus playlists matched to your energy state and task type. Each playlist is curated to support a specific kind of mental state.

Step 3

Set your session length

Choose the session length that fits your task and energy. Shorter sessions lower the barrier to starting; longer sessions let you build real momentum.

4

Step 4

The AI coach checks in

FlowOwl, the AI accountability coach, speaks to you at three key moments during every session. It helps you notice drift, stay on task, and reflect honestly on your progress.

Before you start — FlowOwl helps you lock in your goal and set your intention for the session.
At the halfway point — A quick check-in to catch drift early and bring you back on track if needed.
After the session ends — A short reflection to close the loop — what did you actually get done?
5

Step 5

Your session history builds

Every session is saved. Over time, FlowSpaceFocus shows you your focus patterns: which playlists work best for you, when your sessions are strongest during the day, and how your average session length is trending. You stop guessing and start understanding how your focus actually works.
