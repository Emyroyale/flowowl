What is FlowSpaceFocus?

Frequently asked questions

What is FlowSpaceFocus? +

FlowSpaceFocus is a web-based AI focus app for distracted and ADHD-prone minds. It combines guided focus playlists, timed work sessions, and an AI accountability coach to help users start tasks, stay engaged, and finish meaningful work.

Is this just a Pomodoro timer? +

No. A Pomodoro timer counts down. FlowSpaceFocus adds an AI coach that prompts you before sessions to define a goal, checks in at the halfway point, and debriefs you after. It also includes curated focus playlists and a session history dashboard.

Is FlowSpaceFocus good for ADHD-prone users? +

Yes. FlowSpaceFocus is designed for people who struggle with task initiation, attention drift, and tab overload. The app provides external structure through timed sessions and uses an AI coach to help users re-enter focus when attention slips. It does not claim to be therapy or medical treatment.

How does the AI accountability coach work? +

The AI coach (FlowOwl) interacts with you at three points: before your session to help you define one specific goal, at the halfway point to check in on your progress, and after the session to reflect on what was completed. It helps you recover from distraction and build focus patterns over time. Available on the Pro plan.

What is included in the free plan? +

The free plan includes focus sessions up to 15 minutes and access to all focus playlists. No credit card required to get started.

What is included in Pro? +

Pro includes unlimited session lengths (15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes), the FlowOwl AI accountability coach, session history, and focus insights. Pro is $9 per month and can be cancelled at any time.

Is FlowSpaceFocus available on mobile? +

FlowSpaceFocus is a web app that runs in any modern browser. It works on desktop and mobile browsers. There is no native iOS or Android app at this time.

Is this therapy or medical treatment? +

No. FlowSpaceFocus is a productivity tool, not a medical or therapeutic product. It is not a substitute for ADHD diagnosis, medication, or therapy. It is a structured focus environment designed to support people who find unstructured work time difficult.
